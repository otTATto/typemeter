Manjari Latin Subset

This application includes a subsetted version of the Manjari font.

Original font:
Manjari, by The Manjari Project Authors / Santhosh Thottingal.

Modification:
The bundled font files have been subsetted to include only Latin characters,
digits, and basic punctuation, and converted to WOFF2 for application bundling.
